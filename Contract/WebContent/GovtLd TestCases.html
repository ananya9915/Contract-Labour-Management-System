1Production site Test Cases:

.I. New Registration Certificate Entry Form:

  1. Ensure that the Work Order Issue Date Should not be Greater than the Current Date
  2. Ensure that the Challan Paid Date Should not be Greater than the Current Date
  3. Ensure that the Mobile No or Phone No Accepts Numerics Only

II. New Work Order Details Entry Form:

  1. Ensure that the Work Order Agreement Date Should Not be Greater than the Current Date
  2. Ensure that the License Issued Date Should Not be Greater than the Current Date
  3. Ensure that the Date Of Work Should Not be Greater than the Current Date
  4. Ensure that the Renewal Date Should Not be less than or equal to the Work Order Issued Date
  5. Ensure that the Renewal Date Should Not be less than or equal to the current date
  6. Ensure that the Security Deposite Paid Date Should Not be Greater than the current date
  7. Ensure that the WelFare Fund Paid Date Should Not be Greater than the current date
  8. Ensure that the Mobile No or Phone No Accepts Numerics Only

III.Renewal of Work Order Form :

  1. Ensure that the Renewal Work Order Agreement Date Should Not be Greater than the Current Date
  2. Ensure that the License Renewal Date Should Not be Greater than the Current Date
  3. Ensure that the Renewal Challan Paid Date Should Not be Greater than the Current Date
  4. Ensure that the License Expiry Date Should Not be less than or equal to the Date of Renewal
  5. Ensure that the Renewal Date Should Not be Greater than the current date
  6. Ensure that the WelFare Fund Paid Date Should Not be Greater than the current date
